Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with us.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collection, add books to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. Contact us with your order number.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! At checkout, you can add gift wrapping for $2.99 per item.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and gift cards.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payments are processed securely. We never store your full credit card information.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express and Priority options are available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select international locations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by country.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items in original condition.

How do I return a book?

Contact us to request a return label, pack the item securely, and ship it back.
